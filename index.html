Welcome to Cee-Lo!!

Rules: Two players roll.

The automatic win combination is any combo of 4-5-6.

Any combination of 1-2-3 is an automatic loss.

Two of the dice must be the same and the differing dice number is your score

For example: a 5-5-1 signfifies a score of 1. a 1-1-5 signifies a score of 5.

If all your dice are different numbers you mus roll till you get a good score.
