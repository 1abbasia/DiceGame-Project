CEE-LO Dice game

Welcome To Cee Lo! The street dice game!

  • Select side and character
  • 4-5-6 is an AUTO WIN!
  • 1-2-3 is an AUTO LOSS!!!
  • Score is the non duplicate roll. Examples:
    • 3-5-5 means a score of 3
    • 1-1-6 means a score of 6
    • 2-2-1 means score of 1
    • 6-6-3 means a score of 3
    • 2-5-6 means you have to roll again until one duplicate
    • 3-3-3 means score of 3
